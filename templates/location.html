Share a map or directions with others


You can share a location, directions, or a map. You can share:
A place, business, or address
A directions search
A Street View image

You can't share:

A map with your saved locations, which are private
A map with a distance measurement
A screenshot or image of the map itself

Android
Share a map or location

On your Android phone or tablet,
open the Google Maps app Google Maps.
Search for a place. Or, find a place on the map,
then touch and hold to drop a pin.
At the bottom, tap the place's name or address.
Tap Share icon.
If you don't see share icon, tap More icon and then Share.
Choose the app where you want to share the link to the map.

  2. Go to the directions, map, or Street View image you want to share.
  3. Select Share or embed map. If you don't see this option, click Link to this map.

  4. Copy and paste the link wherever you want to share the map.
